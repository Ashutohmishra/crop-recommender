Knowledge Base

Kissan Tips & Guidance

🧪
Soil Health Tip
Test soil pH every 2 years. Ideal range for most crops is 6.0–7.5. Apply lime if below 5.5, gypsum if above 8.0.
💧
Water Management
Drip irrigation saves 40% water vs flood method. Install soil moisture sensors at 15 cm depth for precision watering.
🌦️
Monsoon Alert
Check our real-time monsoon prediction before sowing. Kharif sowing window is June 5–25 for most Indian states.
🐛
Pest Control
Use pheromone traps from day 30 after sowing. Early detection reduces pesticide use by 60% and saves crop losses.
🌿
Organic Boost
Apply Trichoderma viride 4 g/kg seed before sowing. Prevents fungal diseases and promotes root growth naturally.
📦
Storage Tip
Dry grains to 12–14% moisture before storage. Use aluminum phosphide tablets in airtight bins to prevent weevil damage.
