Smart Crop Guide
{% if result == "rice" %}
  • 💧 Water: Maintain 5–10 cm standing water; irrigate every 3–4 days if dry.
  • 🌱 Soil: Clayey or loamy, pH 5.5–6.5 for best retention.
  • 🌡️ Temperature: 20–35°C with high humidity ideal.
  • 🧪 Fertilizer: Urea (N), Phosphorus for roots, Potassium for strength.
  • 🌿 Organic: Compost, green manure, cow dung improve fertility.
  • 🐛 Pests: Stem borer, brown planthopper, leaf folder.
  • 🧫 Diseases: Rice blast and bacterial leaf blight.
  • 🌿 Bio-control: Neem oil spray every 10–15 days.
  • 📈 Monitor: Check tillering ~25 days after planting.
  • 🌾 Harvest: 110–130 days; grains turn golden yellow.
  • 🔁 Rotation: Follow with lentils, chickpeas, or vegetables.
  • 💰 Yield: 3–6 tons/hectare.
  • 🧑‍🌾 Tip: Level the field and ensure drainage to avoid stagnation.
{% elif result == "banana" %}
  • 💧 Water: Irrigate every 2–3 days during hot seasons.
  • 🌱 Soil: Fertile loamy soil, pH 6.0–7.5.
  • 🌡️ Temperature: Tropical climate 26–30°C ideal.
  • 🧪 Fertilizer: Nitrogen (leaf growth) and Potassium (fruit).
  • 🌿 Organic: Farmyard manure, compost, vermicompost.
  • 🐛 Pests: Banana weevil and nematodes.
  • 🧫 Diseases: Panama disease, leaf spot.
  • 🌿 Bio-control: Neem cake and Trichoderma.
  • 📈 Monitor: Observe fruit bunch formation weekly.
  • 🌾 Harvest: 10–12 months after planting.
  • 🔁 Rotation: Vegetables or legumes after harvest.
  • 💰 Yield: 30–40 tons/hectare.
  • 🧑‍🌾 Tip: Use support poles against heavy wind.
{% elif result == "maize" %}

  • 💰 Yield: 4–8 tons/hectare.
  • 🧑‍🌾 Tip: Maintain spacing for sunlight and air circulation.
